## SECTION 1 — WHAT DIGNITY IS TODAY

### 1.1 Public Narrative Summary

Dignity Gold presents DIGau as a blockchain-based digital security token backed by U.S. mining reserves. The public-facing story carries meaningful institutional signals:

- DIGau is described as a **security token**, not a utility token or payment stablecoin
- Issuance is framed under **Regulation D / Regulation S** exemptions
- Investor eligibility requires **KYC/AML screening** and on-chain risk gate passage
- Securities distribution is described as offered through **Tritaurian Capital** (a registered broker-dealer)
- **Dignity Corp. itself is not a registered broker-dealer** — the BD wrapper is the distribution rail
- Reserve assets described as U.S. mining properties with NI 43-101 studies referenced
- Structure references pledge documentation, UCC liens, and deeds of trust

### 1.2 What the Public Story Supports

| Signal | Public Support | Caveat |
|--------|---------------|--------|

### 1.3 What the Public Story Does Not Prove

- End-to-end transfer restriction and transfer agent controls

- LP/market-maker infrastructure

- Solvency and minimum reserve maintenance proof
- Monthly investor and regulator reporting

### 1.4 Honest Characterization

**What it is now:** A compliance-forward security token positioning with credible leadership signals, a broker-dealer distribution relationship, and a mining reserve narrative.

**What it is not yet:** A fully validated, institutionally auditable, operationally proven RWA platform with closed compliance gaps.

## SECTION 5 — STABLECOIN RAILS

*See also: DIGNITY_STABLECOIN_RAILS_INTEGRATION.md*

### 5.1 Why Stablecoin Rails Matter

USDC and USDT settlement rails:
- **Accelerate subscription settlement** — no ACH clearing window
- **Enable global investor reach** — non-U.S. investors without USD bank accounts
- **Strengthen LP operations** — market makers can fund inventory in stablecoins
- **Improve treasury velocity** — reserve contributions can move intraday
- **Support OTC settlement** — RFQ desk can settle in USDC/USDT T+0

### 5.2 Compliance Separation

**Critical:** The GENIUS Act stablecoin framework governs **payment stablecoins** (USDC/USDT). DIGau is a **security token**. These are legally distinct. Adding stablecoin rails to Dignity is a **payment and treasury tooling decision**, not a securities law change. Both compliance tracks must be maintained in parallel.

### 5.3 Payment Rail Compliance Requirements

- FinCEN/MSB analysis for stablecoin-denominated flows
- OFAC sanctions screening on all stablecoin transactions
- Travel Rule compliance where applicable (≥$3,000 transfers)
- State money transmission licensing exposure analysis
- AML transaction monitoring — separate from DIGau compliance monitoring
