## THE ASSET

**DIGau** is a digital security token backed by U.S. gold and mineral reserve assets. The token is structured as a security (not a utility token or payment stablecoin), issued under Regulation D and Regulation S exemptions, distributed through Tritaurian Capital (a registered broker-dealer), and restricted to investors who pass KYC/AML screening and on-chain wallet risk controls.

The reserve assets are described as U.S.-domiciled mining properties with NI 43-101 reserve studies. The legal structure references pledge documentation, UCC liens, and deeds of trust protecting token-holder interests against the underlying assets.

**Honest assessment:** The public narrative is directionally correct. The documented evidence base — reserve title depth, legal opinion volume, insurance confirmations, transfer agent controls, and audit trail completeness — is the area requiring systematic build-out.
